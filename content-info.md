## About FI
Status: about

### Ominaisuudet
- **Kategorisoitu sisältö**: Selaa aihepiireittäin

- **Latausvaihtoehdot**: Vie linkkejä, kirjanmerkkejä ja PDF-tiedostoja

## About EN
Status: about

This is a bilingual (Finnish/English) resource collection designed to help people find useful information and services.

### Features
- **Categorized Content**: Browse by topic areas
- **Multi-language Support**: Content available in Finnish and English
- **Download Options**: Export links, bookmarks, and PDFs
- **Dark Mode**: Comfortable viewing in any lighting
- **Responsive Design**: Works on all devices
